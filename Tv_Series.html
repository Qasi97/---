Tv Series

  • Lucifer

    Lucifer Morningstar has decided he's had enough of being the dutiful servant in Hell and decides to spend some time on Earth to better understand humanity. He settles in Los Angeles - the City of Angels.

  • Viking

    Vikings transports us to the brutal and mysterious world of Ragnar Lothbrok, a Viking warrior and farmer who yearns to explore - and raid - the distant shores across the ocean.

  • Daredevil

    A blind lawyer by day, vigilante by night. Matt Murdock fights the crime of New York as Daredevil.

  • Iron Fist

    A young man is bestowed with incredible martial arts skills and a mystical force known as the Iron Fist.

  • The Flash

    After being struck by lightning, Barry Allen wakes up from his coma to discover he's been given the power of super speed, becoming the Flash, fighting crime in Central City.

  • Riverdale

    While navigating the troubled waters of romance, school and family, Archie and his gang become entangled in dark Riverdale mysteries.

  • La Casa de Papel

    An unusual group of robbers attempt to carry out the most perfect robbery in Spanish history - stealing 2.4 billion euros from the Royal Mint of Spain.

  • After Life

    After Tony's wife dies unexpectedly, his nice-guy persona is altered into an impulsive, devil-may-care attitude; taking his old world by storm.

  • The Walking Dead

    Sheriff Deputy Rick Grimes wakes up from a coma to learn the world is in ruins and must lead a group of survivors to stay alive.

  • Black Lightning

    A crusading school principal gets back into action as the original African-American electrical superhero.

  • Supernatural

    Two brothers follow their father's footsteps as hunters, fighting evil supernatural beings of many kinds, including monsters, demons and gods that roam the earth.

  • Breaking Bad

    A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future.
